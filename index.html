Lazy I Beerworks was born out of a shared passion for good, fresh, and unique brews & food shared with friends. We've embarked upon a journey to share this passion with our community in Central Montana and beyond. We serve familiar bites at Lazy I Beerworks; bringing together unique brews with great food, atmosphere and friends. Follow our journey from vision to reality!
